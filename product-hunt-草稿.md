## 产品描述 (260 字限制)

> CoinPing is a Telegram bot that monitors crypto prices so you don't have to stare at charts. Unlike simple price-ping bots, it supports three alert types: price thresholds (crossing a level up/down), 24h % change (catch volatility without watching), and cross-exchange spread (spot wicks and liquidity events). Set your conditions once, close the chart, and get pinged on Telegram when they hit.
>
> Built in Go with zero recurring costs — CoinGecko free API, SQLite, Render free tier. Free to use with 3 active alert slots per user.
